Description

Time to add some information about your amazing product/service prices. Add two new sections inside the main tag. The first section will feature a title and a text; the second section will be more peculiar.

In most frontend libraries, there is a component called card that presents a neat way to show content on websites. Take the Bootstrap library as an example. In the second section, build similar cards and show the prices of your product/service. For the details of these sections, have a look at our Figma designs. Pay attention to the CSS rules and the elements in the designs. The element names are the names of the tags you must use. You might notice that some elements have different naming principles than in the previous stage, such as div: .card. Here, the tag name is div, and the class is card.

You can choose to write what you like for the text content for these sections. There should be three cards in the second section.

Align the text content of these cards in the second section and the h1 and the paragraph in the first section to the center.

Moreover, note the element structure in Figma; it hints at your HTML element structure. The elements' positions should be close to Figma designs. You can adjust the position of the elements however you like. You can pick to change the font.

Not only that, but you can also play around with the colors of the elements.

Objectives

Take a look at the designs in Figma. In this stage, build only the design from Stage 2: Pricing information.
