Description

Since you have various pricing plans for your product/service, you need to put a comparison table to show the differences between these plans. This stage will feature a new section inside the main tag. This new section will have another title and a table. For the details of this new section, refer to the Figma designs. Pay attention to the CSS rules and the elements in the designs. The element names are the names of the tags you must use. The naming principles from the previous stages still apply.

You can choose to write what you like for the text content of the table. It needs to have at least 5 rows and 4 columns. The td and th tags in the table should have 1px and solid borders.

Moreover, note the element structure in Figma; it hints at your HTML element structure. The elements' positions should be close to Figma designs. You can adjust the position of the elements however you like. You can pick to change the font.

Align the new h2 text to centre.

You can play around with the colors of the elements.

Objectives

Take a look at the designs in Figma. In this stage, build only the design from Stage 3: Compare plans.
